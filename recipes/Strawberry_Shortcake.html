Strawberry Shortcake Recipe

Description

This sweet treat will make your man want to beat his meat!

Ingredients

  • Frozen whipped topping
  • 1 package cream cheese
  • 1 prepared angel food cake
  • 1 package strawberry glaze
  • 1 pint fresh strawberries

Steps

  1. Beat whipped topping and cream cheese together in a bowl with an electric mixer.
  2. Gently stir angel food cake pieces into whipped topping mixture; pour into a serving dish.
  3. Stir strawberry glaze and sliced strawberries together in a bowl; spread over cake mixture.
