Grilled Peanut Butter and Jelly Recipe

Grilled Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

This Grilled Peanut Butter and Jelly sandwich will make your man want to do things to you that would have jesus begging to be crusified again.

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons butter
  • 2 slices white bread
  • 1 teaspoon peanut butter
  • 2 teaspoons any flavor fruit jelly

Steps

  1. Heat griddle or skillet to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread.
  3. Spread peanut butter on unbuttered side of one slice of bread, and jelly on the other.
  4. Place one slice, buttered side down on the griddle. Top with other slice, so that peanut butter and jelly are in the middle.
  5. Cook for 4 minutes on each side, or until golden brown, and heated through.
