Creamy Hot Chocolate

Description

This mouthwatering brown sludge will make you cuckoo for cocoa puffs!

Ingredients

  • 1 (14 ounce) can EAGLE BRAND Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 6 1/2 cups hot water
  • 3 10 miniatures Mini marshmallows

Steps

  1. In large saucepan over medium heat, combine sweetened condensed milk, cocoa,
    vanilla and salt; mix well. Slowly stir in water. Heat through, stirring occasionally.
    Do not boil. Top with marshmallows, (optional). Store covered in refrigerator.
